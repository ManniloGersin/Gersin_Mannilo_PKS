# Cybersecurity Notes

## Red Team
The Red Team simulates cyberattacks to test an organization's security defenses. Their role is to identify vulnerabilities, exploit weaknesses, and assess how well the system can withstand real-world attacks.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct penetration testing
- Simulate phishing and social engineering attacks
- Identify system vulnerabilities
- Test incident response readiness

---

## Blue Team
The Blue Team is responsible for defending and monitoring systems against cyber threats. They focus on detection, prevention, and response to security incidents.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor network activity
- Detect malicious behavior
- Respond to incidents
- Implement security controls
- Improve defensive strategies
